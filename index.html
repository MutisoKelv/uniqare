About Us

We are committed to providing quality services to patients at all times.

Uniqare Medical Laboratories has been in existence for over 23 years under the name Arrow Medical Laboratory which was re-branded the year 2012 as Uniqare Medical Laboratories with the aim of bringing change in the industry.


Uniqare Medical Laboratories is a full-fledged Registered and Licensed Medical Laboratory with different branches which are fully equipped with highly qualified and experienced Medical Laboratory Technologists and Technicians from accredited medical training institutions.

Services

Consultation

on medical laboratory and diagnostic services including that of diagnosis of human ailments and components of the body and pathological investigations.

Diagnostic laboratories

Establishing medical diagnostic laboratories for purposes of health enhancement.

Health & diagnostic education

We offer Health and diagnostic education services and more so participate in current Research Activities, Quality Control Programmes and Health Care Services.

Medical checkup

We carry out laboratory medical check up for food handlers within the region

DNA Testing

We conduct DNA testing in partnership with CSI Nairobi.

Medical examination

We conduct medical examination for people who wish to travel outside the country for either job placements or those joining higher education colleges/universities. For the past years we have conducted medical examination for people travelling to Dubai, Qatar, Afghanistan, Kuwait, Lebanon, U.S, Germany, among others.
